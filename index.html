Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers. Everything you need to know about DevConf 2026.

Can I get a refund if my plans change?

Yes, we offer full refunds up to 14 days before the event starts (August 5, 2026). After that, you can transfer your ticket to a colleague at no extra cost.

Is there a group discount for teams?

Absolutely! Our Team package offers a discounted rate for up to 10 attendees. If you have a larger team, contact us for custom corporate pricing.

Will the sessions be recorded?

Yes, all keynotes and main track sessions are recorded. Standard and Pro pass holders get access to digital recordings for 30 days post-event.

Can I expense this ticket to my company?

Yes. Upon registration, you will receive a formal invoice and tax receipt that you can submit to your finance department for reimbursement.

What hardware do I need for workshops?

For the hands-on tracks, you will need a laptop with a modern web browser and a solid internet connection. Specific setup instructions will be emailed a week before the event.

Are the mentorship sessions 1-on-1?

Yes, the 30-minute mentorship sessions included in the Pro tier are private, 1-on-1 consultations with our featured industry experts and speakers.
